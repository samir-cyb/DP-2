This use case diagram illustrates all the functionalities available to users in the Justice Link BD application:

Key Features:

  • Reporting System: Submit reports with text, audio, or image attachments
  • Timeline: View all reports with reaction-based sorting (Dangerous, Suspicious, Normal, Fake)
  • Notifications: Receive and respond to emergency alerts with navigation
